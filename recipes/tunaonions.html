Tuna and Onions

About the dish

Forged by those ones who don't have the time or money necessary to make the usual meals, they came up with a dish capable of amazing flavors and textures and quick preparation times. Tuna and onions has been the go to option for the fish lovers of Peru.

Ingredients

  • A can of tuna
  • 1 or 2 onions depending on the size
  • 1/2 cup of rice
  • seasoning

Preparation

  1. Make your rice the asian way and add some extra oil and salt to the water before start cooking
  2. Chop the onions in any style you prefer,keep in mind that they don't need that small
  3. On a hot oiled pan fry the onions until they acquire a browinish tone
  4. Add your seasonings to the onions, it could be salt, pepper or msg, no limits
  5. Now open the tuna can and put it with the onions, get rid of the extra oil if you mind the calories
  6. Constantly stir and try to get the onions to grow more brown
  7. Put the rice on a plate and the tuna on the side, pour out the extra oil of the pan before doing this
Enjoy your meal :)
